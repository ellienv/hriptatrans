About us

Hripta V.I. and LLC "I.M.A.-Elit Trans" - a transport company founded in 1996 and provides services of transportation of goods. The company transports goods both across Ukraine and in international directions. Our own fleet includes about 30 modern Euro-5 and Euro-6 Mercedes-Benz, trucks Kögel and Schmitz semi-trailers, including 18 - awning ones and 15 - reefer trucks. Trailers carrying capacity is 22-25 tons and they are presented for transportation:
  • food products
  • cargo, requiring temperature regime from +20 to -20 degrees Celsius
  • cargo volume up to 94 cubic meters
  • consolidated cargo
  • personal and diplomatic goods
  • argo that is ADR (dangerous))

In a short period of time, our company has become a leader in transportation in Ukraine and Europe.

Team Hripta V.I. and LLC "I.M.A.-Elit Trans" consists of more than 30 qualified employees, whose goal is to organize the most qualitative process of cargo delivery. The movement of vehicles and their status is monitored in real time using the GPS monitoring system.

The company Hripta V.I. and LLC “I.M.A.-Elit Trans” are constantly working to improve services in the field of international transport of goods, the fleet is constantly updated, the staff undergoes systematic training for professional development.

The activities of Hripta V.I. and LLC "I.M.A.-Elit Trans" implements on the basis of valid licenses, received in the prescribed manner. The carrier’s liability insurance (CMR insurance) is issued for each individual shipment, so you can be completely sure about the integrity of your cargo.
